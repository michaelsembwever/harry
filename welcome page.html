Welcome Message

My decision to run for Mayor is all about making a difference to the future direction of our beautiful region, to protect the history, the natural environment and our productive farmlands. Looking to the future we need a Council administration that is streamlined and is held accountable to a high performance standard and good governance. The Council managers and workforce need an environment in which they are given appropriate responsibilities and respect to do their jobs to the best of their abilities. Creating a sustainable future for our region is about having a Mayor and Council which actively seek new opportunities , encourage new business which give the region long term employment growth while protecting and enhancing the unique qualities of our region.
