Your Support

Dr Harry needs your support to become Mayor. It is time there is a fresh energy brought to the job of Mayor of the Scenic Rim. The Region needs vision, hard work and forward thinking to meet it's potential. Your support is very important to me, please go to the contacts page to leave contact details for me to discuss how we can work together.

Caring For Our Region

I am passionate about our wildlife and our beautiful natural environment. I am also very concerned about land use management, protecting farmlands for future food security. I will always be an advocate for protecting the rights and the values of our residents. To this end I think the Council administration needs to be more transparent and accountable and there needs to be a change in the "culture" of the workplace at Council.

Growth and Planning

The Region is an area of great natural beauty, tourism will be a major part of the Region's growth and employment opportunities. The Bromelton transport hub will become a reality and will provide an enormous opportunity to attract business to the Region. It will only become a reality if it is treated as a project that is pushed along with energy and imagination. Business growth needs to be balanced with good planning, including protecting farmlands, natural habitats, and the amenity of the residents affected by development.
