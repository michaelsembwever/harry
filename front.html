Caring For Our Region

I am passionate about our wildlife and our beautiful natural environment. I am also very concerned about land use management, protecting farmlands for future food security. And I will always be an advocate for protecting the rights and the values of our residents. To this end I think the Council administration needs to be more transparent and accountable and there needs to be a change in the "culture" of the workplace at Council.

Growth and Planning

The Region is an area of great natural beauty, tourism will be a major part of the Region's growth and employment opportunities. The Bromelton transport hub will become a reality and will provide an enormous opportunity to attract business to the Region. It will only become a reality if it is treated as a project that is pushed along with energy and imagination. Not just a photo opportunity evry 4 years just before election time. Business growth needs to be balanced with good planning, including protecting farmlands, natural habitats, and the amenity of the residents affected by development.
